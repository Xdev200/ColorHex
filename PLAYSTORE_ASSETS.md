## 3. Visual Style Guide (RetroUI)

All assets were generated following the **Neo-Brutalist** design system established for ColorHex:
*   **Colors**: Vibrant Primary Yellow (#FAFF00), Neon Green, and Pink.
*   **Typography**: Bold 'Outfit' and 'Inter' font stacks.
*   **Borders**: Thick, solid black borders (3px–10px depending on scale).
*   **Shadows**: Hard, non-blurred 3D shadows for a "sticker" aesthetic.
