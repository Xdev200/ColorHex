How to Play

01. Study

You'll be shown a color swatch for a few seconds. Memorize the shade and its subtle details.

02. Match

When the grid appears, find the exact match among multiple variations. No hints, just your eyes.

03. Level Up

Each level increases the difficulty—more colors, closer shades, and faster timers.

Game Levels

Phase 1: Pure Match

The foundation. Master high-precision color matching with single hex codes across 32 increasingly complex levels.

Phase 2: Duo Blend

Double the challenge. Identify shades created by blending two distinct HEX colors. A true test of spectral depth.

Phase 3: Trio Master

The ultimate frontier. Predict the outcome of three blended colors. Only for those with a perfect eye for detail.
