Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ColorHex ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play our game.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The game may store the following locally on your device:

Any data stored is used exclusively to improve your local gaming experience. We do not transmit your scores or progress to external servers unless you explicitly sign in to a third-party leaderboard service (like Game Center or Google Play Games).

If you use third-party services within the app, their respective privacy policies apply. We recommend reviewing the policies of Apple and Google regarding game services.
