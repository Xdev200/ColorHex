How to Play
1
Study the Color A large circle shows a random color. You have a few seconds — memorise it!
2
Find the Match The circle disappears. Pick the exact matching color from the grid.
3
Level Up Each level adds more color options — from 2 up to 64! The faster & fewer attempts, the higher your score.
4
Unlock Phases Complete all 32 levels to unlock Phase 2 (blend of 2 colors) and Phase 3 (blend of 3 colors).
Scoring: 1000 base pts + time bonus − attempt penalty
Tip: Wrong answers reveal the correct color briefly!
